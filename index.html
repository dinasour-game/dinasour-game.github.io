About Dinosaur Game

The Dinosaur Game is a classic endless runner where you guide a T-Rex through a desert, dodging obstacles like cacti and pterodactyls. Originally a hidden Easter egg in Google Chrome's offline mode, it has become a beloved game for its simplicity and addictive gameplay. Jump and duck to survive as long as possible!
